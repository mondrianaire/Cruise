Day-by-Day Itinerary

7 Nights

The wider trip starts in Montreal on Jun 11 and ends with two nights in Boston after the cruise. The 7-night sailing itself begins in Québec with two sea days down the St. Lawrence, then four port days through Atlantic Canada and Maine before a morning arrival in Boston.

Dark pins mark embarkation (Québec) and disembarkation (Boston). Brass pins are ports of call. The sage pin and the brown dashed line show the pre-cruise leg from Montreal, and the navy strip at the bottom-left marks the two-night post-cruise stay at the Boston Harbor Hotel before flying home Jun 21.
Pre • Thursday, June 11

Arrive Montreal

Group converges at YUL • Humaniti Hotel
Becca & Sandra land at YUL late morning and pick up the rental. Jett, Laura & Karen land in the evening. Check in at the Humaniti (Autograph Collection) and dinner in Old Montreal.
Pre • Friday, June 12

Drive Montreal → Québec City

~145 km via Autoroute 20 • Les Lofts St-Pierre
Scenic drive day with stops at Trois-Rivières and Parc des Chutes-de-la-Chaudière. Check in at Les Lofts St-Pierre in Old Québec; return the rental at YQB by evening.
Day 1 • Saturday, June 13

Quebec City, Quebec — Embarkation

All aboard during the afternoon • Ship departs 11:00 PM
Board the ship and enjoy a late sail-away past the floodlit Old City. An optional embarkation-day tour of Historic Quebec & Montmorency Falls is offered (see Ports below).
Day 2 • Sunday, June 14

Cruising the Saint Lawrence River

Scenic cruising — no port call
A full sea day. Great for settling in, booking spa treatments, and exploring the ship as the shoreline drifts by.
Day 3 • Monday, June 15

Cruising the Gulf of St. Lawrence

Scenic cruising — no port call
Open-water cruising into the Gulf. Watch for marine life — the Gulf is rich whale habitat in June.
Day 4 • Tuesday, June 16

Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island

In port 7:00 AM – 5:00 PM
The longest port day — ten hours ashore on Canada's smallest province.
Day 5 • Wednesday, June 17

Sydney, Nova Scotia

In port 7:00 AM – 4:00 PM
Gateway to Cape Breton Island, the Cabot Trail and the Fortress of Louisbourg.
Day 6 • Thursday, June 18

Halifax, Nova Scotia

In port 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM
Nova Scotia's capital. Reminder: Pinnacle Grill dinner is booked for 6:00 PM (Laura).
Day 7 • Friday, June 19

Portland, Maine

In port 12:30 PM – 10:00 PM
An afternoon-and-evening call — ideal for a lobster dinner ashore and Old Port strolling at dusk.
Day 8 • Saturday, June 20

Boston, Massachusetts — Disembarkation

Arrives 7:00 AM • Boston Harbor Hotel check-in 3 PM
Voyage ends. Disembark in the morning, then explore Faneuil Hall / Freedom Trail before checking in to the Boston Harbor Hotel for the post-cruise night (a birthday night for Jett).
Post • Sunday, June 21

Fly home

Boston Harbor Hotel check-out 12 PM • BOS→PHL 12:32 PM
Hotel check-out by 12, then Silver Line or Uber to BOS for the 12:32 PM flight. Jett & Laura connect through PHL on (3:20 PM) to GSO.
