Your Package & Onboard Credits

Have It All

Both Jett and Laura are booked on Holland America's premium Have It All package. Here is what is already included before you spend a cent on board.

🍷 Signature Beverage Package

Cocktails, wine, beer, soft drinks and specialty coffees included up to the daily limit, for both guests.

🍽 Specialty Dining

One specialty restaurant dinner per guest — already redeemed.

📷 Shore Excursion Credit

$100 per guest ($200 for your room) toward shore excursions. Applies automatically to the highest-priced tour in your cart.

📱 Surf Wi-Fi Package

Internet for browsing, email and social media throughout the voyage, for both guests.

💰 Onboard Credit

$85 per stateroom in spendable onboard credit — use it for the spa, extra dining, gift shop or gratuities.

✅ Crew Appreciation

Daily gratuities are typically billed to your onboard account — confirm at check-in whether they are prepaid.

Spend the excursion credit wisely. The $200 room credit is applied to your single highest-value tour. If you book one bigger excursion and one smaller one, put them in the same cart — the discount lands on the pricier ticket automatically.
