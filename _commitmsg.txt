v.133: uniform 230x1449 deck canvas - preserve bow-to-stern proportions

v.132 cropped each deck PNG to its own content bbox. That fixed the
horizontal whitespace but broke the relative scale: short top decks
(10 with bbox 197x1132 and 11 at 197x1064) displayed at the SAME
visual height as full-length cabin decks (1-5 at ~220x1448) when
rendered at max-height:88vh. A user looking at Deck 11 saw a ship
just as long as Deck 5 - physically wrong.

Fix: horizontal-only crop to a uniform canvas. Restored originals
from /deck-originals/, then cropped every deck to (15, 0, 245, 1449)
- a 230x1449 canvas wide enough for the widest ship outline plus
4-6px padding either side, preserving the full original height.

Result: all 11 decks share the same canvas dimensions, so they
display at the same scale. Short decks retain their whitespace at top
and bottom because that whitespace is the physically empty bow/stern
portion - Deck 11 ship outline now visibly occupies only the upper
~80% of its canvas, correctly shorter than Deck 5's full-length ship.

Cache buster -> ?v=133 on styles.css, app.js, venues.js, and every
deck PNG reference.
