v.140: drop duplicate deck dropdown, snap above selector, fix alignment

Three fixes called out in feedback:

1. Removed the .fp-deck-mobile <select> dropdown — it duplicated
   the 1-11 pill row directly underneath it. The pill row is the
   only deck picker now (and works on every viewport).

2. snapToDeckchart() now scrolls so the cross-section / deck-plans
   selector sits just below the page title instead of pinned to
   the very top of the viewport. Title stays visible above the
   selector. Triggered on view-tab clicks too, not just on
   orientation flips.

3. Walking-path overlays + hotspot bounding boxes were both
   misaligned with the deck PNGs:

   ROUTE PNGS — re-generated all 11 route-NN.png from the source
   HAL accessibility-routes PDF panels. The bug: when computing
   the rotated panel's content bbox, the deck title text ("DECK
   11—SPORTS DECK") became a vertical strip down the left side
   after rotation and inflated the bbox. Fix: blank the title rows
   in the pre-rotation panel before bbox detection, then scale
   the resulting ship-hull bbox independently on X and Y to fit
   each deck PNG's actual content x AND y bbox. Routes now sit
   inside the hull on every deck. Verified Deck 11 — bow access
   route reaches the observation lounge, stern route reaches the
   Sport Court with the stairlift indicator at the entry.

   HOTSPOT BBOXES — historic fp-hot percentages assumed the ship
   filled the canvas, but after the v.135 tight crop short decks
   (especially 10, 11) have the deck content positioned partway
   into the canvas. Remapped all 49 hotspots to fit within each
   deck's actual content y/x range. Examples:
     Deck 11 Retreat:    3.0% / 22.0% -> 10.9% / 16.0%
     Deck 11 Sport Court: 80.0% / 14.0% -> 67.0% / 10.2%
     Deck 10 Crow's Nest: 3.0% /  7.0% -> 10.1% /  5.4%

   Asset versions bumped to ?v=140; SITE_VERSION = 'v.140'.
