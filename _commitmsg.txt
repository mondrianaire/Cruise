v.123: rotation also resets iOS pinch zoom

window.scrollTo only affects the layout viewport - iOS Safari's pinch
zoom lives on a separate visual viewport that scrollTo can't touch.
Added resetPinchZoom() to snapToDeckchart():

  - Briefly set the viewport meta to
      width=device-width, initial-scale=1, maximum-scale=1, user-scalable=no
    which forces iOS to snap back to 1x scale.
  - 250ms later restore the original viewport meta so the user can
    pinch again later.

This is the only reliable way to programmatically reset visual-
viewport zoom on iOS Safari short of a page reload. Effect: every
rotation now lands you at 1x zoom, scroll position (0,0), then the
deckchart anchored to the top of the viewport.
