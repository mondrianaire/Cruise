v.116: orientation gate shows on every portrait load

Removed the sessionStorage.orientationGateDismissed suppression. The
rotate-to-landscape modal now appears on every portrait load — not just
the first one of the session. Landscape still auto-dismisses it
visually, and the "Use deck plans (portrait) instead" escape hatch
still works for the current load (it just no longer remembers the
choice across reloads).
