v.113: blocking orientation gate + snap-to-top on rotation

On initial load on mobile/tablet in portrait, the ship page now shows a
full-screen orientation gate:
  - animated phone glyph + curved rotate arrow
  - "Rotate to landscape" headline
  - explanation that cross-section view is built for landscape
  - small "Use deck plans (portrait) instead" escape hatch

The gate dismisses the moment the device flips to landscape (no manual
action required). The escape hatch sets the override flag, switches to
deck plans, and dismisses. Desktop (>=1101px) never sees the gate.

Rotation behavior:
  - sessionStorage.shipViewOverride no longer blocks autoOrientView -
    rotation is now authoritative.
  - On every orientation change the view snaps to the very top of the
    deckchart container so the user always lands at the top of the
    new view.
  - Landscape switches to cross-section + snaps.
  - Portrait switches to deck plans + snaps.
