v.124: tap-to-select venues + kill iOS blue selection markers

Touch handler rewrite:
  - Was: long-press (>=350ms) toggles .is-touched; quick tap navigates.
  - Now: tap fires .is-touched immediately on touchstart, runs the click
    handler in parallel, clears after 1.4s. No hold required.
  - Removed the touchend e.preventDefault() and the touchmove cancel
    path that came with the long-press model.

iOS selection-marker suppression on every venue / deck-plan touch
target (.fp-hot, .dv-item, .dc-svg, .md-zone, .cz, .fp-deck-img, etc.):
  -webkit-tap-highlight-color: transparent  (no blue flash)
  -webkit-touch-callout: none               (no "Copy" / share callout)
  -webkit-user-select: none + user-select   (no text selection)
  -webkit-user-drag: none on .fp-deck-img   (no image drag)

Rules are inlined in ship.html's critical CSS block AND mirrored in
styles.css so they apply regardless of cache.
