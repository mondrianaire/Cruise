v.120: iOS-bulletproof rotate overlay (no animation, solid bg, !important)

Body.overflow:hidden was firing (view froze) but the overlay itself
stayed invisible on iOS. Rebuilt the overlay CSS as defensively as
possible:

  - Default: display:none !important.  Visible: display:flex !important.
    The cascade cannot overrule it.
  - Solid rgba(4,19,28,.96) background directly on the overlay element.
    Dropped the separate .ro-backdrop child (and its backdrop-filter:blur
    which iOS Safari sometimes refuses to render). .ro-backdrop kept in
    markup but rendered display:none.
  - 100% width/height instead of inset / explicit edges.
  - box-sizing:border-box so padding doesn't push it off-screen.
  - z-index bumped to 99999.
  - All animations removed - direct show/hide. The card no longer pops
    in either; it just appears. No animation = no possibility of iOS
    leaving us stuck mid-keyframe at opacity 0.
  - overflow:auto + -webkit-overflow-scrolling:touch so a short card
    still works on tiny landscape phone heights.
