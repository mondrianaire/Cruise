   55 to 60 (now x=450..510). 5px gap between bubbles preserved.
   Sub-emoji glyphs re-centered within both new bubble widths.

   Dining Room, World Stage) now have stroke-linejoin/linecap:round,
   stroke-miterlimit:2, and paint-order:fill-stroke in the inline

   style instead of rendering as sharp angled paths.
