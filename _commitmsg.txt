v.112: Night Owl works on polygon containers; mobile cross-section polish

Bug fix - reported: Night Owl mode no longer applied to containers
bordering an edge of the ship. The dim / 24h-glow / late-night rules
only matched <rect>, so polygon-edge containers (Retreat, Crow's Nest,
Fitness, Sea View, Dining Room, World Stage) were unaffected. All four
night-owl rules now match both rect AND path.

Mobile screenshot fixes:
- LIDO POOL: the 🍔 🍕 🍹 emojis were sitting at y=138, exactly on
  top of the "enter Deck 9" subtitle text. Moved them up to y=100
  (inside the glass-roof portion of the bubble where the pool is
  visible from Deck 10), so they have their own row clear of the
  label + sub.
- Top tap-hint ("Tap any amenity...") was unstyled and spread
  across the diagram. Now a compact pill positioned absolute at
  top:4px, low-contrast, ellipsised on narrow viewports.
- Bottom hint "Tap deck numbers on the left..." (a ::after pseudo
  on .dc-svg-wrap) was colliding with the fixed bottom-left
  Animation toggle + Replay intro toolbar on landscape mobile.
  Hidden below 900px.
