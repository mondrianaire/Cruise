Getting from Montreal to Quebec City. You land in Montreal (YUL) on June 11 and stay at the Humaniti Hotel (Autograph Collection by Marriott), then move on to your loft in Quebec City before the ship departs June 13. Montreal to Quebec City is roughly a 3-hour drive (~250 km) or about 3¼ hours by VIA Rail train — plan that intercity transfer. Hotel details are in Where You're Staying.
Disembarkation day. The ship arrives in Boston at 7:00 AM on June 20, but your flight home is June 21 — you will need one hotel night in Boston, which is not yet booked. Boston Logan (BOS) is about 15–20 minutes from the cruise terminal. See Where You're Staying.
