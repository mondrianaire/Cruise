Know Before You Go

Practical Info

Documents & Check-In

  • Complete Online Check-In by June 10, 2026 in the Navigator app or on hollandamerica.com.
  • This is a cross-border cruise (Canada & USA): bring a valid passport — the recommended document for all guests.
  • Print or save your boarding pass and luggage tags after check-in.
  • Note any medical needs and emergency contacts in your profile.

Weather & Packing

  • Mid-June in Atlantic Canada / New England: daytime highs typically 60–72°F (16–22°C), cooler at sea and in the evenings.
  • Pack layers: a warm jacket, light rain shell and a sweater — coastal weather shifts fast.
  • Comfortable walking shoes for cobblestones (Quebec) and granite (Peggy's Cove).
  • Smart-casual evening wear; one dressier outfit for a Pinnacle / specialty dinner.
  • Sunglasses, sunscreen, refillable water bottle, daypack and any medications.

Money & Currency

  • Canadian ports (Quebec City, Charlottetown, Sydney, Halifax) use the Canadian dollar (CAD).
  • US ports (Portland, Boston) use the US dollar (USD).
  • Cards are widely accepted; carry a little local cash for small vendors and tips ashore.
  • Onboard purchases bill to your stateroom account — settle it before disembarking in Boston.

Onboard Tips

  • Book spa treatments and specialty dining early — pre-cruise discounts apply.
  • Your Signature Beverage Package and Surf Wi-Fi are active from embarkation.
  • Watch the daily schedule in the Navigator app for shows, talks and Music Walk sets.
  • Shore excursions can sell out — coordinate and book the group's tours together soon.
  • Ship's clock stays on one time zone; all aboard times in this hub are local port time.
